About us

Divine District Assembly is the restoration arm of Mityana District and is a non-profit assembly representing the citizens of Mityana to bring development at the community level.

We are on mission to set up community development assemblies across Mityana District, create new standards for communities to implement the restoration plan on the basis of natural law.

What Are Community Assemblies?

Our Community development assemblies serve as platforms for citizens to come together, discuss their needs, and collaborate on actionable plans that address local issues. By harnessing collective wisdom, we can create meaningful change.

No boss but associations of equal people who want to transform the lives of the residents of their various communities for the better.
